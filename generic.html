My Academic Career

I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science and Technology from the School of Engineering (SOE) at Cochin University of Science and Technology (CUSAT). While my formal academic journey provides me with a solid foundation in computer science, my passion for technology goes beyond textbooks and lectures.

  • School Project: Python & MySQL Integration

    As part of my academic coursework, I embarked on a project to create a movie ticket booking software. This hands-on experience allowed me to apply my programming skills in Python and explore database management with MySQL. It provided a practical perspective on the software development process.

  • Hardware Enthusiast

    Beyond the classroom, I have nurtured a deep interest in hardware projects. I have undertaken a variety of projects, One of my most notable achievements is the creation of a miniature working model of a Tesla car. This project was a labor of love and earned me the first prize in a science exhibition. It reflects my dedication to innovation, attention to detail, and the ability to bring technological ideas to life.
